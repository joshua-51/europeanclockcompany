Antique Clock Repair and Restoration

Basic Maintenance and Servicing

  • Routine Cleaning and Oiling – Remove dust and old oil, re-lubricate pivots and gears.
  • Inspection and Diagnosis – Complete assessment of wear, damage, and missing parts.
  • Adjustments – Regulate timekeeping, level clocks, and set beat for accuracy.

Movement Overhaul and Restoration

  • Complete Movement Overhaul – Disassembly, ultrasonic cleaning, pivot polishing, re-bushing, and reassembly.
  • Gear Repair and Replacement – Repair worn teeth or replace broken wheels or pinions.
  • Spring and Weight Work – Replace or re-tension mainsprings, inspect weight cables and pulleys.
  • Bushing and Pivot Work – Install new bushings and polish pivots for smooth operation.

Chime and Strike Work

  • Chime Train Adjustment – Repair or time Westminster, Whittington, or other chime sequences.
  • Strike Train Repair – Ensure hour and half-hour striking works correctly.
  • Bell, Gong, and Hammer Alignment – Improve tone quality and synchronization.

Case and Dial Restoration

  • Case Cleaning and Refinishing – Waxing, French polishing, or refinishing wood cases.
  • Dial Restoration – Clean, re-silver, repaint numerals, or restore enamel dials.
  • Glass and Bezel Repair – Replace broken glass, polish bezels, or repair hinges.
  • Brass Polishing – Restore brightwork: bezels, finials, pendulum bobs.

Specialty Services

  • Pendulum Repair and Replacement – Adjust length, repair suspension springs, or fabricate new pendulums.
  • Escapement Work – Re-face pallets, adjust verge depth, restore proper beat.
  • Custom Part Fabrication – Create missing wheels, arbors, or decorative pieces.
  • Automation Repairs – Fix music boxes, cuckoo mechanisms, and automata.

Consultation and Extras

  • Appraisal and Valuation – Written appraisals for insurance or sale.
  • Clock Setup and Moving – Safe packing, transportation, and setup.
  • Education and Instruction – Teach owners how to wind, set, and care for clocks.
