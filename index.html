A Journey Through Time

What began in the oil business evolved into a lifelong passion. The founder of The European Clock Company retired from his previous career to become a dedicated clock maker and fixer. Having lived and worked in Vienna, Austria, and traveling frequently between Vienna and Salt Lake City, a deep appreciation for European craftsmanship was forged.

Today, that rich history translates into an unparalleled expertise in horology. Beyond clocks, our founder brings a diverse array of experiences to the table, from spending time in Hawaii to the meticulous art of beekeeping. Every clock we touch is handled with the same patience and precision.
