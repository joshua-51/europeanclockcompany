A Journey Through Time

Founded out of a passion for horology and a commitment to preserving history. The interest in timepieces began while living in Vienna, Austria, which ultimately led to the collection of more than 50 antique clocks from around the world.

After retiring from a career in the oil business, this lifelong passion transitioned into a dedication to clockmaking and repair. Today, I take great pride in my meticulous attention to detail and my dedication to excellence in every restoration project.

The Collection

Repair

Curation
